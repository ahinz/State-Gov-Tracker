Welcome to State-Gov-Tracker

We have command line scripts done to:

  • Get a token from Cicero
  • Get a user's address, pass it to Cicero, and get information back for who their legislators are
  • Get that official's Facebook posts, Twitter tweets, and a geo-specific Google News search for their name

      • We Also have a database schema, wireframes, and a collected database of sponsorships and votes, gathered through the Sunlight OpenStates API. We made that our own database so we can serve results to the user quicker.
