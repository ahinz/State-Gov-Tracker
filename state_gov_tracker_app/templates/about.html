What we Do

MyRep.is serves as an information dashboard for Pennsylvanian citizens to monitor their state representatives and senators. The goal is to allow constituents to monitor the activity of their representatives to increase accountability. To that end, MyRep.is seeks to provide not only raw information similar to websites and services like OpenStates, Project Vote Smart, and Follow the Money - which we rely on for some of our data - but to also provide context to this information for Pennsylvanians. Much of the data on our site is already out there; however, we feel it is hard for average citizens to aggregate and monitor the activities of their state representatives.

Future Plans

This is only the beginning for MyRep.is - we have a number of ideas for how to help Pennsylvanians become better informed about politics in Harrisburg. Over the coming weeks, months, years, decades, and centuries we have a number of new features and plans in the works.
Browse by Topics
Right now information for representatives is ordered chronologically. In the next few weeks we also want to organize information by topics to let you browse the activity of your representatives according to the issues you value most.
User Registration and Voting
We wish to allow users of MyRep.is to register with screenames and allow them to express their approval or disapproval of their representative's actions through "digital votes." These votes can serve as a running tally on your state representatives for when it comes time to vote.
